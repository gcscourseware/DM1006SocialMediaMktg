Defining Your North Star: KPIs

Not all data is created equal. **Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)** are the metrics that actually correlate with your business objectives. We categorize them into the **Social Media Funnel**.

Awareness (Top Funnel)

Reach, Impressions, Share of Voice.

Engagement (Middle Funnel)

Likes, Comments, Shares, Save rate.

Conversion (Bottom Funnel)

CTR, Conversion Rate, ROI/ROAS.

Native Insights

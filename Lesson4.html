Campaign Optimization & A/B Testing

The Optimization Loop

Successful advertisers never "set and forget." They use A/B testing (Split Testing) to let the market decide which ad is best.

AD A
Lifestyle Image

Showing a person using the product in a park.

CTR: 1.2%

AD B
Product Close-up

White background focusing on features.

CTR: 2.8% (Winner)

Next Step: Pivot

Since Ad B won, we now allocate 80% of budget to close-ups and test new "Headline" variations for Ad B.

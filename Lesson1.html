What is Social Media Marketing?

Social Media Marketing (SMM) is the process of creating content for social media platforms to promote your products and/or services, build community, and drive traffic to your business.

Traditional View

Broadcast a message to as many people as possible (One-way communication).

Social Marketing View

Build relationships and engage in a dialogue with customers (Two-way communication).
